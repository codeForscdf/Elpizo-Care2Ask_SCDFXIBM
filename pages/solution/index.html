AskOps Chatbot

Our first solution aims to address the issue that some of our paramedics might need more information regarding how to deal with cases at scene and they need it fast. We therefore present Ask SOP, an application that has a chatbot equipped with all the protocols for medical, trauma and HAZMAT emergencies. On top of this, we also equipped the chatbot with data about Emergency Medical Service operating procedures for those who hold secondary appointments in the force. Medify

MedTalk is an application for people to translate hard to understand documents into simplified text. We are trying to use OCR technology for these people to upload their documents and have the text extracted from them. However, the high volume of text uploaded might be translated into highly inaccurate pieces, and hence, we have a platform for these users to upload the particular words or phrase they can't comprehend and we hope our translations will ease their confusion.
