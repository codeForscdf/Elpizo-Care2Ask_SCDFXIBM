Manning365

To run an emergency response force for all 31,536,000 seconds of the year, a roster has to be put up. Currently, there are many different roster ICs, each in charge of the rosters for regulars, NSFs and ORNSmen. Every month, they have to painstakingly calculate the hours each personnel has worked to allocate meal allowances accordingly, as well as take note of their shift patterns, swaps between Rotas or even stations, and VLs or MCs left. Hence there is a need for multiple roster ICs to tend to these administrative matters on top of their main duty to respond to emergency calls.
